# ff-idea-validation

Validate your app idea before wasting months building the wrong thing. Uses search demand signals, assumption mapping, and pretotype experiments to tell you whether your idea has real demand - or if you're building into a void.

Built for vibecoders - people who build apps with AI tools (Cursor, Replit, Bolt, Lovable) but skip the "does anyone want this?" step.

## Skills (6)

- **validate-idea** - Check if real people are searching for what you want to build. Uses keyword demand signals and SERP analysis to score demand as Strong, Moderate, or Weak.
- **keyword-demand-check** - Deep-dive a specific keyword or phrase. Checks Google SERPs, classifies competition level, and tells you if you can rank for it.
- **idea-scorecard** - Score your idea on a 0-1.0 scale using 7 evidence-based demand factors. Turns gut feeling into a confidence number.
- **idea-flip** - Reposition your idea when demand exists but your framing doesn't match how people search. Finds the right words for the right product.
- **assumption-check** - Identify the riskiest assumptions about your idea across demand, usability, money, and feasibility. Finds what could kill your product before you build it.
- **pretotype-design** - Design a cheap, fast test to validate demand without building the full product. Based on Alberto Savoia's pretotyping methodology.

## Commands (1)

- `/ff-idea-validation:validate` - Run a full validation cycle: demand check, assumption mapping, and pretotype design in one workflow. Produces a complete validation report.

## Who This Is For

- You built an app last weekend and want to know if anyone will use it
- You have 5 ideas and don't know which one to build first
- You shipped something and got zero sign-ups - now you want to understand why
- You want to validate before you build, not after
