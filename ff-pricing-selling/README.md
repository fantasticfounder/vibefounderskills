# ff-pricing-selling

Price your product and learn to sell it. Pricing model selection, monetization strategy, sales page structure, offer creation, and objection handling for founders who built something great but don't know what to charge or how to sell it.

Built for vibecoders who can ship a SaaS in a weekend but have zero business training on pricing or sales.

## Skills (5)

- **pricing-strategy** - Choose the right pricing model and set your first price. Covers flat-rate, tiered, per-seat, usage-based, freemium, and lifetime deals. Uses Van Westendorp, value-based pricing, and the 10x rule.
- **monetization-model** - Evaluate how to make money from your app beyond just subscriptions. Scores 8 monetization models on predictability, effort, alignment, and scalability. Includes evolution paths.
- **sales-page-structure** - Structure a landing page that converts visitors into users or customers. Uses AIDA, PAS, and StoryBrand frameworks. Produces section-by-section copy recommendations.
- **offer-creation** - Create a compelling offer that makes the price feel like a no-brainer. Uses Alex Hormozi's value equation from "$100M Offers." Builds value stacks, risk reversals, and urgency mechanisms.
- **objection-handler** - Handle the common objections founders face when selling their product. Uses Chris Voss's tactical empathy from "Never Split the Difference." Produces a ready-to-use objection playbook.

## Commands (1)

- `/ff-pricing-selling:price` - Run a full pricing and selling workflow: monetization model, pricing strategy, offer creation, sales page structure, and objection handling. Produces a comprehensive Pricing & Selling Strategy document.

## Who This Is For

- You built something and have no idea what to charge
- You've been giving it away for free because pricing feels scary
- You need a sales page but don't know what to write
- You hate sales but need to convince people to pay
- You want to create an offer that sells itself, not just list features and a price
