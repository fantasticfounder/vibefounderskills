# ff-launch-acquisition

Get your first users after building your product. Strategic market entry planning, channel selection, launch execution, and community-based acquisition - designed for solo founders and small teams.

Not just "post on Reddit" advice. Real strategy: market segmentation, channel-market fit analysis, acquisition funnels, and systematic outreach.

## Skills (6)

- **first-users-plan** - Create a strategic plan to get your first 100 users. Covers market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM), beachhead selection, channel-market fit, acquisition funnels, and week-by-week execution.
- **channel-scorer** - Score and rank acquisition channels for YOUR specific product. Uses 6-factor channel-market fit analysis instead of generic "best channel" advice.
- **launch-checklist** - Step-by-step launch plan with pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch phases. Covers timing, messaging, channel coordination, and realistic platform assessments.
- **cold-outreach-draft** - Write cold outreach messages that get responses. Personalisation frameworks, message structures, and follow-up sequences for email, DMs, and community messages.
- **beachhead-segment** - Find your first market segment using Geoffrey Moore's Crossing the Chasm framework plus competitive strategy filters. Includes expansion path mapping.
- **community-marketing** - Build a sustainable presence in communities where your users hang out. Covers contribution strategy, content calendars, and the 90-day community presence roadmap.

## Commands (1)

- `/ff-launch-acquisition:launch` - Run a full launch strategy: beachhead segment -> channel scoring -> launch plan -> outreach -> community strategy. Produces a comprehensive launch document.

## Who This Is For

- You built a product and have zero users
- You've tried posting about your app but got no traction
- You need a real acquisition strategy, not a list of social media tips
- You want to understand market segments, channels, and growth before spending months on the wrong approach
