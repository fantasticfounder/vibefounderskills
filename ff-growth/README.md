# ff-growth

Grow and retain users after launch. Growth loops, referral systems, email list building, retention auditing, north star metrics, and scaling assessment - designed for solo founders who got their first users and want more.

Not "go viral" advice. Real growth strategy: sustainable loops that compound, retention diagnostics that find the leak, and scaling plans that tell you what breaks at 10x.

## Skills (6)

- **growth-loops** - Design sustainable growth loops (not one-off hacks) that compound over time. Evaluate 5 loop types for product fit, score them, design mechanics, and build a 30-60-90 day implementation plan.
- **referral-system** - Build a referral system that actually generates new users. Covers referral types, incentive design, mechanics, tracking, and realistic targets for indie products.
- **email-list-strategy** - Build an email list from scratch as the foundation of sustainable growth. Lead magnet design, capture points, 5-email welcome sequence, and ongoing strategy.
- **retention-audit** - Find out why users leave and what to do about it. Stage-by-stage retention diagnosis, habit loop analysis (Nir Eyal "Hooked"), and a prioritised improvement plan.
- **north-star-metric** - Define the ONE metric that best captures the value your product delivers. Candidate evaluation, input metrics, dashboard spec, and targets.
- **scale-audit** - Assess whether your one-person business can scale - and what needs to change if it can't. Time allocation breakdown, bottleneck analysis, unit economics projection, and scaling roadmap.

## Commands (1)

- `/ff-growth:grow` - Run a full growth strategy workflow: define metrics, design growth loops, build retention, and plan for scale. Produces a comprehensive Growth Strategy Report.

## Who This Is For

- You shipped a product and got some users, but growth has stalled
- You want sustainable growth mechanisms, not one-off hacks that spike and die
- You need to understand why users leave before you spend more on acquisition
- You want to know if your business can scale - or if it's better as a lifestyle business
- You're a vibecoder who builds with AI tools but needs real business strategy
