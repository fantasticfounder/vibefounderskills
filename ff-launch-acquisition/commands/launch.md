# /launch -- Full Launch Strategy

Build a complete launch strategy from scratch: identify your first market segment, score and select channels, create a launch plan, and draft outreach messages - all in one workflow.

## Invocation

```
/launch AI-powered meeting notes app for freelancers
/launch Shopify analytics dashboard for small store owners
/launch [paste your product description]
```

## Workflow

Carry forward outputs between steps. Beachhead segment name and rationale from Step 2 feeds into Step 3. Channel recommendations from Step 3 feed into Step 4. If the user changes direction at any checkpoint, re-derive all downstream steps.

### Step 1: Understand the Product

Ask:
- What does your product do? (one sentence)
- Who is it for? (be specific)
- Is it live? Can people sign up today?
- What's your pricing model?
- Have you done any marketing yet? What happened?
- Do you have any existing users or waitlist?

Accept context from uploaded files, competitor research, or conversation.

### Step 2: Find Your Beachhead Segment (~15 min)

Apply the **beachhead-segment** skill:

- Brainstorm 4-6 candidate segments
- Score each on: pain intensity, willingness to pay, reachability, market size, referral potential, competitive landscape
- Apply strategic filters: bowling pin expansion path, minimum viable segment, founder-market fit
- Recommend ONE beachhead segment with clear justification

**Checkpoint**: "Here's my analysis of your market segments. I recommend targeting [segment] first because [reasons]. Does this feel right, or should we explore a different segment?"

### Step 3: Score Acquisition Channels (~10 min)

Apply the **channel-scorer** skill:

- Evaluate 8-12 channels against: audience presence, discovery intent, content-format fit, competitive saturation, effort-to-reach ratio, founder fit
- Rank channels by total score
- Recommend primary (50% time), secondary (30% time), and experimental (20% time) channels
- Flag channels to avoid and why

**Checkpoint**: "For [beachhead segment], the best channels are [1, 2, 3]. Here's why. Should I build the launch plan around these, or do you want to adjust?"

### Step 4: Build the Launch Plan (~20 min)

Apply the **launch-checklist** skill:

- Check launch readiness (product, landing page, analytics, messaging)
- Design pre-launch phase (2-4 weeks)
- Plan launch day execution (hour-by-hour schedule)
- Design post-launch momentum plan (4 weeks)
- Set success metrics and contingency plans

### Step 5: Draft Outreach Messages (~15 min)

Apply the **cold-outreach-draft** skill:

- Write outreach messages tailored to the beachhead segment
- Create channel-appropriate versions (email, DM, community post)
- Design a 3-touch follow-up sequence
- Build a personalisation guide

### Step 6: Design Community Strategy (~10 min)

Apply the **community-marketing** skill:

- Map the community landscape for the beachhead segment
- Design a contribution strategy (80% value, 20% product)
- Create a weekly engagement schedule
- Set 30-day and 90-day community presence goals

## Notes

- Allow 90-120 minutes across multiple exchanges
- At each checkpoint, the user can redirect, skip, or go deeper
- The report is designed to be a living document - update it as you learn
- If the user hasn't validated demand yet, strongly recommend running /validate first
- Be honest about channel predictions. No channel is guaranteed to work.
